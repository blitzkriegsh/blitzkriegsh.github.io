Rules

  • The competition is based on IT and computer science.
  • A team consisting of two participants from a college can compete in the competition.
  • The quiz consists of two rounds:
    • Preliminary Round: Includes 21 questions based on PowerPoint presentations.
    • Mega Final Round: Consists of 6 different rounds, all based on PowerPoint presentations.
  • Top 5 teams from the preliminary stage will qualify for the mega finals.
  • In the mega finals, questions will cover textual, visual, audio, video, and connection types.
  • There are star (*) marked questions to break ties, and those who answer them will receive bonus points.
  • Negative points will be given in the mega finals for wrong answers.
  • The competition concludes with the top 2 teams after the final round being declared as the winners.
