About Blitzkrieg

Department of Computer Science, Sacred Heart College (Autonomous), Thevara, organises an Inter Collegiate IT Fest, BLITZKRIEG 2024 on WORLD COMPUTER DAY (15th February, 2024). ‘BLITZKRIEG 2024’ which is an intellectual merge of events to explore the technical aspects of students. It allows them to exhibit their skill, ability, and talent. It aims at bringing out the talent in students and honours them for what they are.
