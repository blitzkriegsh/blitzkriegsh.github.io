About Blitzkrieg 2024

Department of Computer Science, Sacred Heart College (Autonomous), Thevara, organises an Inter Collegiate IT Fest, BLITZKRIEG 2024 on World Computer Day (15th February). ‘BLITZKRIEG 2024’ which is an intellectual merge of events to explore the technical aspects of students. It allows them to exhibit their skill, ability, and talent. It aims at bringing out the talent in students and honours them for what they are.

Sacred Heart College (Autonomous)

Sacred Heart College (Autonomous), Kochi is an institution of higher education affiliated to the Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam. It was established in 1944. In the year 2000, the college was accredited by the NAAC and reaccredited with A+ level on 2022. The college offers 21 undergraduate and 20 postgraduate programmes, 1 Integrated programme and a range of certification courses with around 3000 students.


The Department of Computer Science began functioning in 2001. It offers two under-graduate programmes BSc Computer Applications and BCA [Mobile Applications & Cloud Technology] started in 2001 & 2016 respectively and an integrated programme, Integrated M.Sc. Computer Science [Data Science] started in 2020.
